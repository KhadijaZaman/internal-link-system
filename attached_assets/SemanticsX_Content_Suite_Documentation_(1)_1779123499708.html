Platform Overview & Core Philosophy

What SemanticsX is and why semantic completeness drives rankings

SemanticsX is an AI-powered semantic SEO platform. Its Content Creation suite is built on a single foundational principle: content ranks when it achieves topical and semantic completeness — covering the full universe of entities, phrases, concepts, and linguistic relationships that Google's NLP systems associate with a topic.

💡
Core Logic Google uses Natural Language Processing (via BERT, MUM, and generative AI) to understand content semantically — not just by keyword matching, but by evaluating which named entities, concepts, and phrase patterns a document covers. SemanticsX reverse-engineers this by extracting those signals from top-ranking competitors and using AI to generate them from scratch.

SEO Rules (Koray Guidelines)

A checklist of 20 writing rules based on Koray Tuğberk Gübür's semantic SEO methodology. Check any rules to enforce them in the AI prompt:

RuleInstruction
🚀 Answer FirstDon't distance the question from the answer. Answer immediately, then elaborate.
🚫 No AnalogiesDon't use analogies that compare one thing to another.
⚠️ Avoid Coreference ErrorsUse clear pronoun references. Avoid ambiguous "he", "she", "it".
🚀 No Extra SentencesCombine sentences when possible to reduce token usage.
✨ If Statements SecondPut "if" conditions in the second part of the sentence.
✨ Match Heading StructureSupporting text should match the heading structure (How to → To do).
📌 Examples After PluralsGive specific examples after mentioning a plural noun.
🚀 Understand Verb ContextUse "increase" for metrics, "improve" for skills/health, "develop" for gradual growth.
🔍 Be SpecificExperts are specific. Say "6 severe symptoms" not just "symptoms".
⚡ Use Numeric ValuesSay "5 main reasons" not "many reasons". Be specific with numbers.
🚀 Cut the FluffDelete contextless words like "Also", "According to", "should know".
⚡ Be CertainState facts definitively. Use "Sun rises every day" not "Sun will rise tomorrow".
📌 Consistent List StructureUse same part of speech at start of each list item.
🎯 Prioritize ContextMatch the interrogative term (where=place, when=time, how=method).
🔧 Multiple Measurement UnitsInclude diverse measurement units (pounds + kg, oz + liters).
✅ Yes/No FirstBoolean questions should start with Yes or No.

How It Works (Logic)

The tool uses server-side proxies to fetch the HTML of each target URL and parses all heading tags (H1–H4). Unique N-Grams Feature

⭐
Generate Unique N-Grams — Generates original, niche phrases that are uncommon in competitor content but topically relevant. This gives your article distinctive phrase patterns that stand out to search engines and help rank for long-tail query variants that competitors haven't covered.

Logic

The tool tokenizes text and counts every co-occurring word sequence from length 1 to 4. Higher frequency counts signal that a phrase is a dominant topic marker in the content. Comparing your article's n-gram profile against competitor profiles reveals which phrases you're under-representing — those are the gaps to fill.

SemanticsX's entire suite is built on one core insight: Google evaluates content semantically, not just by keyword density.
